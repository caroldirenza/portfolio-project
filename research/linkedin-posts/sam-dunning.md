When NOT to do SEO/AEO (3 real scenarios for SaaS).

1. You're a new category
If your offer or sector isn't mature or well known by people then the chances are it won't have much demand to capture. This means there will be next to no people searching on Google/LLMs for the offering because people simply don't know it's a thing. A better option in this case is to talk to your target customers and learn where they go to get their trusted information and consume content.

2. You need results yesterday
SEO is rarely super quick, unless you operate in a tight niche or have little competition. If you're under huge pressure from investors to hit sales targets in a couple months, you may be better of looking at paid ads and outbound to drive appointments faster. We typically see SEO impact 90 days from publishing pages.

3. You love spending $$$ on ads & hate growth
SEO is solid for building mind-share from prospects constantly seeing your brand recommended in LLMs or #1 in googles organic results. It'll scale organic traffic, re-enforce other channels, ramp up retargeting options, build trust in the b2b ecosystem, improve sales leads, and lower reliance on paid media.
