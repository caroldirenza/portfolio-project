I think we're building the best community for B2B VPs / CMOs.

CMOs always tell us it's lonely at the top. You'd think that when you climb the ranks, you have all the support needed to do your job successfully, but it's actually the opposite that happens. No one on your team understands your world because you are dealing with more strategy vs execution. And the other executives in the company aren't marketers.

This leaves you feeling lonely. It can make you unsure of your decisions. And also feeling blind to what else is going on out there.

That's why peer communities like the Exit Five CMO Council exist. It's the only space online (and in-person) where you can meet new CMO friends, talk shop, and just vent about career and company-related stuff. We have 60+ members today: all VPs and CMOs, leading the department, doing >$50M in revenue per year.
