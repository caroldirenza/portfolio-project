It's the end of an era: I've exited Refine Labs.

Last week, I sold all my remaining shares in Refine Labs to existing shareholders and a private investment firm for an undisclosed amount. This marks the close of a chapter in my life and career that I will always look back on with deep appreciation for the journey, pride in what we built, and excitement for what's next.

When I started Refine Labs from my South Boston apartment in 2019, I had three freelance customers, no employees, and $3,000 in my bank account. No investors. No social media presence. No impressive resume. Just a vision:
- To create the place where future B2B CMOs are forged.
- To design growth systems for the most iconic and emerging brands in SaaS.
- To push the boundaries and move the industry forward.

Refine Labs became exactly that. And far more.

Over six years, we partnered with 250+ leading SaaS companies. We helped forge a new wave of CMOs who are both strategists and tacticians. Refine Labs alumni have gone on to lead Marketing teams at top brands or build companies of their own. Thousands of podcast listeners used the insights to get promoted, reimagine their careers, and drive impact in their orgs.
